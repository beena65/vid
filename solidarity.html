Solidarity Farmers’ Campaign

The Solidarity Farmers’ Campaign is a movement that aims to support and empower small-scale farmers by connecting them with consumers and fair market prices for their goods. The campaign promotes agro-ecological and organic farming practices, and encourages consumers to buy directly from local farmers to strengthen their community and the local economy. By supporting the Solidarity Farmer Campaign, consumers can play a vital role in ensuring that small-scale farmers have the resources and support they need to continue providing healthy, locally-sourced food. Learn more about the local farmers in your area, and start supporting them by buying their products. Help create a sustainable food system that benefits both farmers and consumers.
