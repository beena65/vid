School Nutrition Awareness Program

Welcome to Sarvaguna Nutrition Awareness Program in Pokhara!

Our program is dedicated to promoting good nutrition and healthy eating habits among children in public and private schools. We believe that every child deserves access to nutritious food, regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Through our program, we aim to educate children about the importance of good nutrition and the benefits it can have on their overall health and well-being. We also work to provide children with access to nutritious food options, as many low-income families may not be able to afford healthy meals.

Some of the key components of our program include:

  • Nutritional education:

    We provide children with information on the importance of a balanced diet, the role of different food groups, and the effects of poor nutrition on health.

  • Cooking classes:

    We offer hands-on cooking classes to teach children how to prepare healthy, affordable meals.

  • Food provision:

    We work with local organizations to provide children with access to nutritious food options, such as fresh fruits and vegetables.

  • Community outreach:

    We collaborate with community organizations, schools and local leaders to promote nutrition awareness and to raise awareness of the importance of good nutrition for children.

    • Our program is open to all children in public schools in Pokhara. We believe that by empowering children with knowledge about good nutrition and providing them with access to nutritious food options, we can help them to grow up healthy and strong.

    If you are interested in learning more about our program or getting involved, please contact us. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of children in Pokhara.
